The Smart Tray

TraySense is a smart surface system that transforms everyday storage into an intelligent monitoring solution. By detecting the presence or absence of items, it delivers real-time alerts that ensure essentials are always accounted for—whether in homes, hotels, hospitals, or retail spaces.

Designed for simplicity and scalability, TraySense brings awareness, efficiency, and control to environments where every item matters.
